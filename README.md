# Maven_market_dashboard
1️. **Project Title**

Maven Market – Sales & Performance Analysis Dashboard

2️. **Short Description / Purpose**

This project focuses on analyzing sales, profit, and return performance for Maven Market to support data-driven business decisions.
The dashboard enables stakeholders to evaluate brand, product, regional, and time-based performance, identify profitability gaps, and monitor return behavior.

3️. **Technology Stack**

- Power BI

- Power Query (data cleaning & transformation)

- DAX (KPIs and calculated measures)

- Dimensional Data Modeling (Star Schema)

4️. **Data Source**

Maven Market retail sales dataset

Includes data related to:

- Transactions

- Products & Brands

- Regions

- Sales, Profit, and Returns
  
(Dataset used for academic / learning purposes)

5. **Features & Highlights**
 
 **Business Problem**

 - Maven Market needed a clear and centralized way to monitor sales performance, profitability, and return behavior across brands, products, and regions.
   Existing raw data made it difficult to identify underperforming products, high return rates, and gaps between target and actual performance.

 **Goal of the Dashboard**

 The goal of this dashboard is to:

 - Provide end-to-end visibility into sales, profit, and returns.

 - Enable comparison of target vs. actual performance.

 - Support data-driven decisions at brand, product, and regional levels.

 **Walkthrough of Key Visuals**

 - KPI Cards: Revenue, profit, profit margin, return rate, and transaction volume for quick performance monitoring.

 - Trend Charts: Time-based analysis to track sales and profit evolution.

 - Geographic Map: Regional performance comparison across markets.

 - Brand & Product Tables: Identification of top- and bottom-performing brands and products.

 - Filters & Drill-Downs: Interactive analysis by time period, region, brand, and product category.

 **Business Impact & Insights**

 - Identified high-risk brands and products with elevated return rates affecting overall profitability.

 - Highlighted performance gaps between targets and actual results to support corrective actions.

- Enabled stakeholders to compare regional performance and optimize market strategies.

- Improved decision-making speed and clarity through a single, management-ready reporting view.
